Snakes are elongated, legless reptiles that belong to the suborder Serpentes. They are found on every continent except Antarctica and vary greatly in size, color, and behavior. Some snakes are venomous, using their venom to hunt and defend themselves, while others are non-venomous and rely on constriction to subdue their prey.
